# Custom Cable Sets

## Your pattern. Your colors. Built by hand.

Every Sundial Audio cable starts from the same foundation: a Canare GS-6 core, Neutrik connectors, hand-soldered terminations, and our signature cloth-over-PVC braid. A custom run lets you decide how it looks — any pattern, any color in our catalog, cut and terminated to your spec.

Outfitting a studio. Matching a touring rig to your brand. Or just building a cable that looks like nothing else on the rack. Tell us what you want, and we'll braid it to order in our Massachusetts shop.

---

## How It Works

1. **Choose a braid pattern** — solid, houndstooth, tracer, zig-zag, and more.
2. **Choose your colors** — any color in our catalog, including special and limited runs.
3. **Choose your cable type and connectors** — instrument, patch, or microphone; straight or right-angle Neutrik plugs.
4. **Give us your lengths and quantities** — one long run cut into however many finished cables you need.
5. **We braid, build, test, and ship.**

**Minimum order:** 100 feet of cable per run, cut into as many finished cables as you like.

## Patterns

- **Solid Color** — a single-hue braid that lets your color choice carry the look.
- **Bungalow** — a heritage pattern drawn from 1920s-era wiring.
- **Houndstooth** — a subtle woven check that adds texture without shouting.
- **Tracer** — classic cloth braid with a contrasting stripe woven in.
- **Two-Color Twisted** — two contrasting braids twisted together for depth.
- **Zig-Zag** — a bold, high-movement pattern that stands out on any stage.

Have something else in mind? We can braid patterns and combinations beyond our standard catalog. Just ask.

## Cable Types & Connectors

- **Instrument & patch cables** — TS–TS or right-angle (RA–TS)
- **Microphone cables** — XLR–XLR
- All terminated with **Neutrik** connectors in straight or right-angle configurations

Not sure what you need? Tell us your gear and your setup and we'll spec it with you.
